##tutorial.greeting
Enemies have been spotted in this sector.
This threat needs to be neutralized, but
first, your colony would need some resources.

##tutorial.camera.keyboard
To pan the camera, use the WASD keys.
The mouse scrolling works near the camera edges
or when the middle mouse button is pressed.
Locate a resource stash by following a marker.

##tutorial1.move.keyboard
The right mouse click sends the selected colony
towards destination.
The colony will fly towards the spot,
but its max jump distance is limited.
