##menu.lobby.game_speed : Game speed
##menu.lobby.game_speed.description
The game speed multiplier.
x1.5 means the game will run 50% faster.
In other words, 10 minutes of the actual gameplay will progress the game time by 15 minutes.

##menu.lobby.fog_of_war : Fog of war
##menu.lobby.fog_of_war.description
This options toggles the fog of war.
If turned on, you won't have an entire map revealed from the start.

##menu.lobby.num_teleporters : Teleporters
##menu.lobby.num_teleporters.description
How many pairs of teleporters will be placed on the map.
If your colony lands on a teleportation platform, it will be transported to the connected teleporter.

##menu.lobby.ui_mode : User interface mode
##menu.lobby.ui_mode.description
Whether to show extra graphical user interface elements or not.
It's easier to play with them, but it's more immersive otherwise.

##menu.lobby.world_resources : World resources
##menu.lobby.world_resources.description
The number of resources the generated world has.
Fewer resources mean it will be hard to play a long battle.

##menu.lobby.world_size : World size
##menu.lobby.world_size.description
The generated world size.
It's usually harder to play on a smaller map.

##menu.lobby.num_creep_bases : Creep bases
##menu.lobby.num_creep_bases.description
The number of drone-producing bases the enemy has.
These bases produce more and more units over time,
but they can't be replaced after being destroyed.

##menu.lobby.creep_spawn_rate : Creep spawn rate
##menu.lobby.creep_spawn_rate.description
This option controls the enemy unit spawn frequency.
It affects the late-game out-of-screen spawns only.
The enemy factories will produce units at the same speed.

##menu.lobby.creeps_difficulty : Creeps power
##menu.lobby.creeps_difficulty.description
The enemy units' power multiplier.
It mostly affects the amount of durability enemy units have.

##menu.lobby.arena_progression : Wave difficulty progression
##menu.lobby.arena_progression.description
This option defines the difficulty progression speed.
Every wave is harder than the previous one and this
option controls by how much.

##menu.lobby.initial_creeps : Initial creeps count
##menu.lobby.initial_creeps.description
How many enemy units will be placed on the map from the very beginning.
This option affects how easy the early game exploration will be.

##menu.lobby.boss_difficulty : Enemy colony power
##menu.lobby.boss_difficulty.description
The enemy colony's power level.
It affects the number of units it will produce and how hard it will be to defeat it in battle.

##menu.lobby.starting_resources : Starting resources
##menu.lobby.starting_resources.description
This defines whether your initial colony will have any starting resources.
Some builds can benefit from the early game advantage.

##menu.lobby.go : Go!

##menu.lobby.infinite_mode : Infinite mode
##menu.lobby.infinite_mode.description
Whether you want to play an infinite arena mode.
In the normal arena, you win after the special wave 20.
In the infinite mode, there is no wave limit.
Survive for longer to get higher scores.

##menu.controls.keyboard.text
Pan camera | Middle mouse button + move, edge scroll or WASD keys
Move colony | RMB click on the destination
Select colony | LMB click on the colony or TAB to switch between colonies
Action card select | LMB click on the card or press 1-5 on a keyboard
Pause | SPACE button toggles the pause
Toggle evolution sheet | ALT button
Exit/Back | ESC button

##menu.controls.gamepad.text
Pan camera | D-pad keys or left stick
Move colony | Right stick click on the destination
Select colony | Right stick click on the colony or L1 to switch between colonies
Action card select | Right stick click on the card or Y, B, A, X and R1 buttons
Pause | START toggles the pause
Toggle evolution sheet | R2 button
Exit/Back | BACK or SELECT button

##menu.overview.classic
>> In Classic game mode, you need to defeat the enemy colony.

>> The enemy has several drone factories; destroy them to decrease the number of attackers.

>> The opposing colony is powerful, make sure you're strong enough before confronting it.

>> Collect resources, increase the influence radius, build colonies.

##menu.overview.arena
>> In Arena game mode, you need to survive the attacks that come in waves.

>> There are 20 waves in total, unless the infinite mode is turned on.

>> Do not attack large groups of enemies: let them spread a little.
