##menu.controls.keyboard.text
[Pan Camera]
\tMiddle mouse button + move, edge scroll or arrow keys
[Move Colony]
\tRMB click on the destination
[Select Colony]
\tLMB click on the colony or TAB to switch between colonies
[Action Card Select]
\tLMB click on the card or press 1-5, Q-T on a keyboard
[Pause]
\tSPACE button toggles the pause
[Exit/Back]
\tESC button

##menu.controls.gamepad.text
[Pan Camera]
\tD-pad keys or left joystick
[Move Colony]
\tRight joystick click on the destination
[Select Colony]
\tRight joystick click on the colony or L1 to switch between colonies
[Action Card Select]
\tRight joystick click on the card or Y, B, A, X and R1 buttons
[Pause]
\tSTART toggles the pause
[Exit/Back]
\tBACK or SELECT button

##game.exit.notice.keyboard

To resume the game, press SPACE.
##game.exit.notice.gamepad
Press the button again to quit.
